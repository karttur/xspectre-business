Thomas Gumbricht

Scientist with 30 years of experience working with spectral data and modelling for natural resources management. Worked 15 years at universities (Sweden and South Africa), and 15 years for UN research organisations in Africa and Asia. Thomas is the inventor of the mathematical-geometric modelling framework for translating spectral signals to contents. He is also a skilled craftsman and designs and builds the hardware.
